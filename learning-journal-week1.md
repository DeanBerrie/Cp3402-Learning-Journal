### Career / Employability / Learning Insights
- After completing the practical, I realised I was a bit out of practice with HTML and CSS, which slowed me down at the start. To improve this, I went back through W3Schools and my previous web design work to refresh my knowledge and fill in areas I had forgotten.
One thing I learned from this week is that my approach to learning needs to improve. I started the practical before fully revising the required content, which meant I was trying to learn and complete the task at the same time. This made the process take longer and reduced how much I actually understood. In the future, I will review the content first before attempting the practical so I can complete tasks more efficiently and with a deeper understanding.
From an employability perspective, this week showed me the importance of keeping technical skills up to date. Even though CMS platforms are commonly used in industry, employers still expect a strong understanding of HTML and CSS for customisation, troubleshooting, and improving user experience. Continuing to develop these skills will make me more confident and better prepared for roles in web design and digital content.
